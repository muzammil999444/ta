Who Are We

A SMALL STUDIO
WITH BIG DREAMS


Games Click is an independent mobile game development studio based in Pakistan. We are a dedicated team of developers, designers, and 3D artists passionate about creating high-quality simulation and open-world gaming experiences for Android users worldwide.

Our games are built with Unity 3D, designed for smooth offline gameplay, and crafted to deliver real fun — from city car simulators and flight games to open-world gangster adventures. We believe great games don't need a big budget — they need passion, creativity, and attention to detail.

What We Do

OUR SERVICES


From concept to Play Store launch — every layer of game development handled with precision.

Game Development

Full-cycle Android game development using Unity 3D — from initial concept and mechanics to final build, testing, and Play Store deployment.

UI/UX Design

Intuitive, visually stunning in-game interfaces. We design HUDs, menus, loading screens, and dashboards that enhance every gaming session.

3D Modeling & Animation

High-quality 3D vehicles, characters, and environments built in Blender — imported into Unity for realistic, immersive gameplay.

Open World & Level Design

Crafting detailed open-world environments, mission structures, and city layouts that keep players engaged and continuously exploring.

Game Mechanics & Physics

Realistic vehicle physics, collision systems, AI behavior, and smooth gameplay controls — making every interaction feel natural and satisfying.

Play Store Publishing & ASO

End-to-end Google Play publishing including store listing optimization, screenshots, and App Store Optimization for maximum visibility.
